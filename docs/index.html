Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about HotStreak

How do I earn coins?

You earn coins by making correct predictions! Win your bets and watch your coin balance grow. You also get coins from the daily bonus wheel, leveling up, completing achievements, and referring friends. New users start with 10,000 free coins.

Is real money involved?

No! HotStreak is completely free to play and uses only virtual coins. There's no real money gambling involved. It's pure sports prediction entertainment designed for fun and friendly competition.

How does the leaderboard work?

Leaderboards rank players by total coin balance. We have daily, weekly, and all-time leaderboards. Climb the ranks by making smart predictions and growing your virtual bankroll. Top players earn special badges and bragging rights!

What sports are available?

We currently support NFL, NBA, MLB, College Football, and Premier League Soccer. NHL is coming soon! Each sport offers full game coverage with moneyline, spread, and totals betting options.

How do odds work?

We use American odds format. Negative odds (like -110) show how much you need to bet to win 100 coins. Positive odds (like +150) show how much you win on a 100 coin bet. Our odds mirror real sportsbooks for an authentic experience.

Can I play with friends?

Absolutely! You can follow friends, compare picks, and see how you stack up on the leaderboard. Create friendly competitions and see who has the best sports instincts. Share your big wins directly from the app!
