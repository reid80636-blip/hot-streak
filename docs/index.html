Features

Everything your family needs, one tap away

From morning routines to dinner plans, FamSync handles the details so you can focus on what matters.

📅

Shared Family Calendar

Month, week, and day views with color-coded events per family member. Syncs with Google Calendar and Apple Calendar.

🦊

Foxy AI Assistant

Your family's personal AI. Ask Foxy to plan dinner, find nearby activities, create events, or manage your grocery list.

📋

Smart Lists

Grocery lists, to-do lists, and custom lists your whole family can edit in real time with priorities and due dates.

📍

Family Location

See where everyone is with real-time location sharing and actual drive time estimates between family members.

🍲

Meal Planning

Plan weekly meals, import recipes, and scan your fridge. Ingredients go straight to your grocery list with one tap.

👪

Family Coordination

Chore rotations, event comments, activity feeds, and a daily briefing keep everyone in sync without the group chat chaos.
